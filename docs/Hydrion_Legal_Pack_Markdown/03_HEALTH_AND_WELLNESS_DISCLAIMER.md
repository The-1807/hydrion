# Hydrion Health and Safety Disclaimer

**Effective date:** July 6, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 6, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0.0

Hydrion is a general wellness and self-tracking app. It helps you log water intake, set personal goals, review progress, receive local reminders, and use gentle challenge mechanics.

Hydrion is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, prevent, monitor, or manage disease. It does not provide medical advice, emergency assistance, clinical supervision, medication guidance, or a professional standard of care.

## Hydration Needs Vary

Hydration needs can vary based on body size, age, activity, climate, altitude, diet, illness, pregnancy, breastfeeding, medications, kidney function, heart function, electrolyte balance, fluid restrictions, and professional instructions.

Hydrion goals, reminders, streaks, achievements, weather adjustments, challenges, analytics, and coaching text are estimates. They may be too high, too low, incomplete, delayed, or unsuitable for your circumstances.

## More Water Is Not Always Better

Excessive fluid intake can be harmful. Do not force yourself to drink water solely to:

- reach an app goal;
- maintain a streak;
- unlock an achievement;
- complete a challenge;
- follow a weather recommendation;
- satisfy a reminder.

Stop, slow down, or ignore Hydrion guidance whenever it does not feel right for you.

## Follow Professional Guidance

Follow instructions from qualified healthcare professionals instead of Hydrion when those instructions apply to you.

Seek qualified advice before relying on a hydration target if you have medical conditions, medication concerns, pregnancy, breastfeeding, fluid restrictions, kidney concerns, heart concerns, electrolyte concerns, blood pressure concerns, swallowing concerns, heat illness concerns, vomiting, diarrhea, fever, significant sweating, or any other health situation that affects fluid balance.

This list is not complete. When in doubt, ask a qualified professional.

## Weather-Informed Goals

Hydrion weather adjustments are general and conservative. They use a bounded product formula and a daily forecast. They are not a medical calculation of your exact fluid needs.

Weather data can be unavailable, delayed, incomplete, or wrong. Location permission, network access, forecast provider availability, and operating system behavior can affect the feature. Notification permission is separate and affects reminder delivery, not whether you can view an in-app weather recommendation.

You remain responsible for deciding whether to accept, adjust, or ignore any recommendation.

## Reminders Are Convenience Notifications

Hydrion reminders are convenience notifications, not safety alerts. They may be delayed, silenced, duplicated, missed, or unavailable because of device settings, battery policies, time zone changes, app suspension, operating system behavior, permission choices, or software defects.

Do not use Hydrion as a medication alarm, emergency warning system, clinical monitoring tool, workplace safety control, or supervision tool for another person's safety.

## Symptoms and Emergencies

Do not use Hydrion to decide whether a symptom is serious. Seek appropriate professional or emergency help for concerning symptoms.

Call local emergency services or seek urgent medical care for severe or rapidly worsening symptoms, confusion, fainting, seizure, severe weakness, breathing difficulty, chest pain, inability to keep fluids down, severe heat illness, or any situation that may be an emergency.

## Your Acknowledgement

By continuing to use Hydrion, you acknowledge that:

- Hydrion is a general wellness tracker;
- Hydrion is not medical advice or a medical device;
- recommendations and reminders are estimates;
- hydration needs vary;
- excessive water intake can be harmful;
- you remain responsible for deciding whether to follow a recommendation;
- professional and emergency guidance should override app guidance.
