# Hydrion V1 Known Limitations

Status: draft, owner review required.

- Terms, Privacy, Health/Safety, and Alpha/Beta legal documents still require
  owner/legal approval before production release.
- The shark startup animation is bundled locally, but creator identity and
  animation-specific licence evidence were not visible from the recovered
  LottieFiles share page during static inspection on 2026-07-07.
- Android APK and App Bundle packaging could not be validated locally because
  the machine has no Android SDK configured.
- iOS build, signing, simulator/device, TestFlight, and App Store Connect
  validation were not run locally.
- Notification delivery still needs real-device validation for permission,
  reboot, timezone, channel, and battery-policy behavior.
- Location/weather mode still needs real-device validation for permission
  prompts, denial flows, forecast success, and forecast failure.
- Production Android signing credentials and store upload approval are not yet
  configured.
- Social sync, connected devices, smart bottle sync, smartwatch sync, HealthKit,
  Health Connect, BLE, and AR are not active V1 capabilities.
- Challenges are local-only.
- Coach, weather personalization, badges, connected devices, wearables, cloud
  services, social services, AR, and advanced coaching are outside the visible
  V1 feature set.
- Public release date, store listing, screenshots, rating questionnaire, support
  process, and public privacy-policy URL are still pending owner decisions.
